Alex Woods, RN, BSN, LNC

CEO

As the CEO of US Legal Nurse Consultants, Alex Woods has six years of extensive clinical ICU nursing experience. She began her career in 2016 with her bachelors in Nursing as a full time ICU nurse and also has years of travel nursing experience in many areas. In 2021, she has earned her certification and been practicing as a Legal Nurse Consultant. Alex and her team will go beyond measures to pursue justice with the attorney and for the client.

Legal Nurse Consultants (LNC) do not practice law although we can serve as the middle man between law and healthcare. We are invaluable to the litigation team in medical cases and participate in the development in trail and depositions preparations and expert testimonies.
