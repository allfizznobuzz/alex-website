Alexandra Woods, RN, BSN, LNC

CEO

Alexandra Woods, CEO of U.S. Legal Nurse Consultants, has six years of extensive Intensive Care Unit (ICU) nursing experience. She began her career as a full-time ICU nurse in 2016 with a Bachelor's of Science in Nursing, from the Capstone College of Nursing, University of Alabama. In addition, Alex has spent years obtaining travel nursing experience, giving her a deeper insight into the medical field and how different hospitals operate. In 2021, Alex earned her certification and began practicing as a Legal Nurse Consultant (LNC).

LNCs do not practice law, although we can serve as the mediator between law and healthcare. Not only are our services invaluable to the litigation team in medical cases, but our participation in trial and deposition preparations will guarantee you the best results for your case.

Services

  • Screen cases for merit
  • Organize medical records
  • Summarize, translate, and interpret medical records
  • Identify and define factors that caused or contributed to alleged injuries/damages

  • Expand the attorney's medical library
  • Provide research studies for the attorney
